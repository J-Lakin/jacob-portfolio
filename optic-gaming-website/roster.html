Optic Gaming Current Roster

Shotzzy
Role: SMG
Anthony "Shotzzy" Cuevas-Castro is a multi-game champion, and rightful legend in both games; Call of Duty and Halo. After becoming a championship player in Halo 5, Shotzzy would join the Dallas Empire and win a world championship in 2020 with that team. Shotzzy is a rising star, with an incredible personality, and a contagious laugh.
Dashy
Role: AR
Brandon "Dashy" Otell is a flex player for OpTic Texas, and as of this season cementing himself as one of the greatest players of all time, and an MVP in Call of Duty Vanguard. Dashy has been a member of OpTic for over 3 years, and brings immense skill, humor, and personality to the team. Dashy may be a professional in Call of Duty, but his skills travel with him to other games, proven by his Radiant ranking in Valorant.
Pred
