Teton Crest Trail

The Teton Crest Trail is one of the most beautiful hiking trails in the United States, running through Grand Teton National Park in Wyoming.

Highlights

  • Scenic mountain views
  • Wildflower meadows
  • Alpine lakes

Trail Info

  • Length: ~40 miles
  • Time: 4-6 days
  • Difficulty: Moderate to strenuous
